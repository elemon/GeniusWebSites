About Me: Joseph Thornton

My name is Joseph Thornton. Im a 15 year old black male highschool sudent, born and raised in Oakland California. I'm going into the 10th grade and starting the path to my career in tech. I'm growing my skill of coding to make the path a little more smooth for me. I'm extremely passionate about gaming which is why I made this site around it. I've been gaming since preschool and my love for games have only grown since then. I want to be able to make a platform for people that are as passionate in gaming as me to get all the info and assistance they need to get the most out of there gaming experience. My goal is to help all gamers!
