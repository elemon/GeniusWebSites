Fighting Game Run Down

If there was any kind of videogame that embodied competive play, it would be a fighting game. This genre thrives on the competitive nature of players and has gained a huge following in the gaming community.

Fighting games are also notorious for their overall difficulty. These games are embeded with complex controls, tech, fundimentals that make the skilled and experienced stand out comletely from the inexperienced. This fact mkaes fighting games a extremely popular genre but also a somewhat, "aquired taste."
